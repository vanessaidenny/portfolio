Freecycle.org
Recycling Services Website

UX UI Interactive Design

Project Purpose

Analyze and propose solutions to enhance the user experience of the current website, Freecycle.org, aiming to optimize its online free trade market and broaden its reach to individuals seeking to recycle items within their communities.

Analysis

Target Audiences

Existing Website

Home Page Screenshot

For the redesign of Freecycle.org, the first step is to conduct a comprehensive analysis of various aspects including the target audience, usability of the existing website, and the current structure, technology, and accessibility. By understanding the needs and preferences of the target audience, we can tailor the user experience to better serve their requirements. Additionally, evaluating the usability of the current website helps identify pain points and areas for improvement, guiding the redesign process. Assessing the existing structure, technology, and accessibility ensures that the redesigned website meets modern standards and provides an inclusive experience for all users. Through this analysis, we can propose solutions to enhance the user experience, optimize the online free trade market, and broaden the reach of Freecycle.org to individuals seeking to recycle items within their communities.

Redesign

Sitemap

By examining the current structure, we can pinpoint any duplicated paths or inconsistencies that may hinder navigation or confuse users. Restructuring the sitemap serves the purpose of enhancing clarity, organization, and efficiency in how information is presented and accessed on the website. By eliminating redundant pathways and ensuring a logical flow from one section to another, we can optimize the user journey and make it easier for visitors to find the information they need. Additionally, a well-structured sitemap improves the website's overall usability and accessibility, making it more inclusive and user-friendly for individuals of all backgrounds and abilities.
