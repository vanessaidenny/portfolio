Key to Thrive
Academic Coaching Mobile App

UX UI Interactive Design

Project Purpose

Research, analyze, and implement a mobile app design tailored for the established brand, with a focus on addressing the study-related needs of students through relevant features.

Design

Mood Board

The chosen mood board option presented to the client has a collaborative and exciting theme, setting the stage for a vibrant style concept characterized by keywords such as dynamic, friendly, and creative. This theme established a foundation of energy and collaboration, utilizing textures inspired by lush and natural elements to create a visually dynamic impact. Introducing a new color, orange, into the existing green and blue palette from the logo provided a split complementary contrast, injecting warmth and liveliness into the atmosphere while emphasizing specific elements. Typography plays a crucial role, employing rounded fonts to evoke a storytelling style, fostering a welcoming and friendly atmosphere with a narrative vibe.

Style Tiles

The style tile combines elements from the chosen mood board with essential technical specifications, integrating user interface elements such as buttons, links, and icons. It meticulously outlines typography details, including font family, weight, and size for each text type, along with providing hex codes for the selected color palette. Serving as a sneak peek into the mobile app's aesthetic, the style tile guarantees a seamless and refined visual identity, ensuring coherence and polish throughout the app's design.

The colors were carefully chosen to ensure the appropriate contrast between font and background colors. For this analysis, we rely on the Web AIM Contrast Checker website, aligning with WCAG standards.

Low Fidelity Wireframes

By utilizing simple shapes, placeholders, and annotations, different screens and interactions were quickly mocked up, facilitating rapid prototyping and iterative design, and enabling the exploration of various design ideas within minutes. This agility allowed for usability testing early in the design process, gathering valuable feedback and iterating on the design promptly. Consequently, usability issues could be identified and addressed upfront, reducing the risk of costly mistakes later on.

Solution

UI Components in Figma

Crafting UI components within Figma for high wireframes serves a dual purpose: to streamline the design process and ensure a cohesive user experience. Firstly, developing a library of UI components establishes a consistent visual language, enhancing aesthetics and promoting user familiarity across screens. Secondly, leveraging Figma's features facilitates efficient iteration and refinement, enabling rapid prototyping and testing of interface configurations. This investment in UI component creation during the high wireframing phase sets a solid foundation for the design journey, prioritizing efficiency, consistency, and user-centricity.

Student Module

The student access module provides a dynamic platform for students to optimize their academic journey. With seamless access to essential resources, including the pillars, time management and organizational tools, and dedicated support services, students can enhance their academic performance effectively.

Administrator Module

The administrator module offers comprehensive tools for managing content and monitoring student progress within the system. Through this module, clients have the ability to oversee various aspects of assessments, strategies, and student performance.
